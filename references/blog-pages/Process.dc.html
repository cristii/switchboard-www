How we work together

Your project, built like a workflow.

Every engagement runs like one of the automations we build for you — a clear sequence of connected steps, each handing clean results to the next. Scroll down to follow the path from first call to a system you own.

↓ follow the wires
Webhook
01 Discovery · the trigger

Every system starts by listening.

A webhook sits quietly until something happens, then fires. Discovery is the same: before we build anything, we listen. We map your current tools, find where work is getting stuck, and agree on exactly what success looks like.

What happens

Discovery sessions, a map of your tech stack, and a clearly scoped project.

The output

A clear blueprint of the system we're about to build — no surprises.

Switch
02 Build & feedback · the switch

We build it — and adjust as we go.

A switch node sends work down whichever path fits the moment. Our build phase works the same way: we develop layer by layer, then branch based on your feedback. Business needs shift — regular check-ins let us route around them instead of grinding to a halt.

What happens

Active development, weekly progress reviews, and quick iterative adjustments.

The output

A working, end-to-end version of your automated system you can try.

Error Trigger
03 QA & testing · the error trigger

We try to break it — on purpose.

An error trigger is the safety net that catches a workflow the moment something goes wrong. Before going live, we deliberately feed your system bad data, dropped connections, and odd user behaviour — so when the real world throws a curveball, it's already handled. (hover the node — that's us catching an error)

What happens

Edge-case testing, load simulation, and fail-safe configuration.

The output

A hardened, resilient system ready for live traffic.

Set
Push
04 Handoff · set & push

We hand you the keys.

The last two nodes in any workflow set the final values and push them where they belong. Handoff is the same: we deploy to your live environment, lock it in, and make sure your team can run it without us. You finish this step owning a system, not renting one.

What happens

Migration to your live environment, hands-on training, and clear documentation.

The output

A fully operational system, under your control.

Schedule
05 Retainer · the schedule trigger

We keep it running on a schedule.

A schedule trigger wakes up at set intervals and does its job without being asked. An automated system is a living thing — rules change, the apps you connect to update, your volume grows. Optional retainers keep everything tuned and humming in the background.
