Small rounded label. Tint variants categorize content (e.g. difficulty levels: green=Beginner, amber=Intermediate, violet=Advanced); the solid orange variant is the "flag" used for callouts like "Most popular".

```jsx
<Badge variant="green">Beginner</Badge>
<Badge variant="amber">Intermediate</Badge>
<Badge variant="violet">Advanced</Badge>
<Badge variant="solid">Most popular</Badge>
```
