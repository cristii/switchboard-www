A small uppercase Bricolage kicker that labels a section, sitting above the H2.

```jsx
<Eyebrow>My services</Eyebrow>
<Eyebrow tone="amber">Why "results" isn't just a word</Eyebrow>
```

Tone `orange` (default, on paper), `amber` (on dark surfaces), `ink`. Often paired with `<HandUnderline>` for the wobble treatment.
